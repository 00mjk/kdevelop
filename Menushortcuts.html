Shortcuts used in KDevelop

This is a collection of tables which may be useful for programmers and translators to find out which characters are already used for shortcuts in menues and dialogs. Mind that the capitalization doesn't matter, i.e. if capital 'A' is used you cannot take 'a' as shortcut.
